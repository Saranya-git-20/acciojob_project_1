The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a breed of small hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. Beagles are intelligent, curious, and known for their amazing sense of smell.

Trivia

  1. Origin: Beagles originated in England around the 1500s.
  2. Snoopy from Peanuts is one of the most famous fictional beagles.
  3. They are known for their loud and distinctive howl.
  4. Beagles have an excellent sense of smell, used in airports to detect food.
  5. They are highly social and require attention and mental stimulation.
